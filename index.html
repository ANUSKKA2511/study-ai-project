Accessibility

Voice Commands

After starting voice control, you can say commands like:

  • "Go to home"
  • "Go to study"
  • "Go to questions"
  • "Go to dashboard"
  • "Go to settings"
  • "Read document"
  • "Summarize document"
  • "Explain document"
  • "Open file picker"
  • "Start dictation"
  • "Stop dictation"
  • "Upload document [name]" or "Upload document"
  • "Load document [name]" or "Load document"

Keyboard Navigation

  • Use Tab and Shift+Tab to move between controls.
  • Use Enter or Space to activate buttons.
  • Use access keys 1–5 to switch pages.
